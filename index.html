— Industrial Methodology

Our Process


Precision engineered manufacturing workflow ensuring quality, durability and consistency at every stage.

01

Phase 1: Solid Wood Frame Construction

A precision-cut solid wood frame with top rail, bottom rail, and stiles ensures structural integrity and load-bearing capacity.

02

Phase 2: Core Filling for Strength

The frame is reinforced with MDF, HDMR, or Hollow-Core Board, enhancing stability, acoustic insulation, and impact resistance.

03

Phase 3: Core Sheet Application

A high-pressure bonded core sheet (HDF, Plywood, or Veneer) ensures surface uniformity, durability, and tensile strength.

04

Phase 4: Laminate or Veneer Finishing

A high-pressure laminate (HPL) or wood veneer is bonded using industrial-grade adhesives, ensuring moisture resistance and a flawless finish.
